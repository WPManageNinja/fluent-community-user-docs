# Posts & Discussions

The core of any community is the conversation. In FluentCommunity, discussions are centered around **Posts**, which act as the starting point for members to share ideas, ask questions, and interact with each other.

This section of the documentation covers all the features you and your members will use to communicate.

## How Discussions Work

* **Posts:** A member starts a conversation by creating a post within a specific **Space**. A post can contain rich text, images, videos, and links.
* **Comments:** Other members can then reply to the post by leaving comments.
* **Replies:** To keep conversations organized, users can reply directly to a specific comment, creating nested threads.
* **Reactions & Mentions:** Members can give quick feedback with emoji reactions or pull other users into a conversation by @mentioning them.

This creates a dynamic and easy-to-follow discussion structure, familiar to anyone who has used modern social media platforms. In the following articles, we will explore how to use each of these features in detail.
