# Course Privacy

Every course in FluentCommunity can be set to one of three privacy levels: **Public**, **Private**, or **Secret**. This lets you decide exactly who can find, view, and enroll in your course content.

> **Use Case:** You want your free "Getting Started" course open to everyone, but keep your premium "Advanced Masterclass" locked behind a paywall with a custom sales page that non-members see before they enroll.

## How to Set Course Privacy

Privacy is configured from inside the course editor, so you'll start by opening the course you want to update.

1.  From the FluentCommunity navbar, open the **Courses** section and click into the course you want to edit. If you haven't built one yet, you can click **New Course** in the top-right corner to start from scratch.

![Opening a course from the Courses section](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/1.-Open-desired-Course-scaled.webp)

2.  In the top-right corner, click the **Edit Lessons** button.

![The Edit Lessons button in the course header](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/2.-Edit-Lessons-button.webp)

3.  Next, click the **Edit** button in the top-left corner to open the course settings.

![The Edit button in the top-left corner of the course builder](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/3.-Edit-button.webp)

4.  Under **General Settings**, open the **Privacy** dropdown. You'll see three options: **Public**, **Private**, and **Secret**.

![The Privacy dropdown showing Public, Private, and Secret options](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/4.-Privacy-settings-option-scaled.webp)

Here's what each privacy level does.

### Public

Anyone can view the course and enroll in it for free. This is the best choice for lead-generation courses or free content you want as many people to find as possible.

### Private

The course is locked for everyone. Only members you add manually (or through automation) can access it, and users can also enroll by [paying a fee](/monetizing-your-courses).

When you select **Private**, you get two **Lock Screen** options for what non-members see:

* **Default:** Shows FluentCommunity's standard lock screen.
* **Custom:** Lets you design your own lock screen. Once selected, open the **Lock Screen** tab from the top menu to access all the customization options.

![Choosing a Custom lock screen type for a private course](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/5.-Custom-lock-screen-type-for-Private-Group-scaled.webp)

#### Customizing the Lock Screen

In the left sidebar under **Page Blocks**, you'll find every block you can customize, including the **Banner**, **Description**, **Lessons**, and **Call to Action**. Each block gives you three controls:

* **Edit:** Click the pencil icon on a block to change its content.
* **Disable:** Click the eye icon to hide a block from the lock screen.
* **Move:** Click the up/down arrows to reorder a block.

![Lock screen customization options in the Page Blocks sidebar](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/6.-Lock-Screen-Customization-options-scaled.webp)

When you click the **Edit** icon on a block, the **Content** tab opens so you can adjust the text:

* **Heading:** Change the heading text.
* **Description:** Change the description text. You can also add a longer description below the banner using the **Description Text** button.
* **Button Label:** Change the wording on your button.
* **Button Link:** Add the URL you want to send students to after they click the button.

![The Content tab for editing lock screen text](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/7.-Content-tab--scaled.webp)

To change the look of the lock screen, open the **Design** tab:

* **Background Image:** Upload an image for the banner background using the **Upload Image** button.
* **Overlay Color:** Add a color overlay on top of the background image.
* **Heading Color:** Change the color of the heading text.
* **Text Color:** Change the color of the description text below the heading.
* **Button Label:** Change the color of the button text.
* **Button Background:** Change the background color of the button.

![The Design tab for styling the lock screen](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/8.-Design-tab-scaled.webp)

When you're happy with the design, click **Save Changes & Close** to keep your changes.

![The Save Changes & Close button](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/9.-Save-Changes-Close-button-scaled.webp)

### Secret

The course is completely hidden from everyone. Only invited students can view and enroll in the content, making this ideal for private cohorts or beta groups.

After choosing your privacy level, always click **Save Changes & Close** to apply your settings.

![Selecting the Secret option and saving changes](/images/course-and-learning/course-privacy/10.-Secret-option-and-Save-Changes-button-scaled.webp)
