# Course Management

This section covers the administrative side of building your online courses. From creating the initial course shell to structuring your lessons and configuring enrollment options, these guides will walk you through every step of the process.

FluentCommunity's built-in LMS is designed to be both powerful and intuitive, giving you the tools to create a seamless learning experience for your students.

### What You Will Learn

In the following articles, we will cover the core aspects of managing your courses:

* **Creating Your First Course:** How to set up the main course page with a title, description, and branding.
* **Structuring Lessons & Modules:** How to organize your content into logical sections and individual lessons.
* **Course Layouts & Enrollment:** How to choose the visual style of your course and define how students access the content over time.
* **Adding Videos & Attachments:** How to enrich your lessons with multimedia and downloadable resources.

Let's begin by creating your very first course.

- Explore [structuring lessons and modules](/structuring-lessons-&-modules) to organize content
