# Course Links

FluentCommunity lets you attach custom **Links** to any course, giving students quick access to related resources, downloads, or external pages directly from the course navigation bar.

> **Use Case:** You're running a photography course and want to add a link to a downloadable presets pack, a private community space, and your YouTube channel, all visible right at the top of the course.

## How to Add a Link to a Course

Links are managed inside the course editor, so you'll start by opening the course you want to add them to.

1.  From the FluentCommunity navbar, open the **Courses** section and click **View Course** on the course you want to edit. If you need a new course first, click **New Course** in the top-right corner.

![Opening a course from the Courses section](/images/course-and-learning/course-links/1.-Open-desired-Course-2.webp)

2.  In the top-right corner, click the **Edit Lessons** button.

![The Edit Lessons button in the course header](/images/course-and-learning/course-links/2.-Edit-Lessons-button-1.webp)

3.  Next, click the **Edit** button in the top-left corner.

![The Edit button in the top-left corner of the course builder](/images/course-and-learning/course-links/3.-Edit-button-1.webp)

4.  Open the **Course Links** tab from the top navbar, then click the **Add New Link** button.

![The Add New Link button under the Course Links tab](/images/course-and-learning/course-links/4.-Add-New-Link-scaled.webp)

5.  A popup appears where you fill in the link details. Once you're done, click **Save**.

![The Add New Link popup with link details](/images/course-and-learning/course-links/Zoro-06-20-2025_04_21_PM-scaled.webp)

### Link Options

Here's what each field in the popup does:

* **Name of the Link:** The label shown for your link. To add an icon beside the name, click the empty box next to this field. A popup lets you set an **Emoji**, **Shape**, **Custom SVG**, or **Custom Image** as the icon.

![Choosing an icon for a course link](/images/course-and-learning/course-links/6.-Icon-Link-scaled.webp)

* **URL:** The destination address users are sent to when they click the link.
* **Enable this Link:** Checked by default. Uncheck it to disable the link and keep it as a draft.
* **Open this link in a new tab:** Also checked by default. Uncheck it if you'd rather the link open in the same tab.
* **Visibility:** Choose who can see the link:
    * **Everyone:** Visible to all users.
    * **Logged-in Users Only:** Visible only to users who are logged into the platform.
    * **By Space/Course Memberships:** Visible only to [members of the courses or spaces you select](/courses-&-learning-(pro)/course-privacy).

Once your links are added, click **Save Changes & Close** to apply them to the course. You can **Edit** or **Delete** any link at any time using its edit/delete controls.

![The Save Changes & Close button after adding links](/images/course-and-learning/course-links/7.-Save-Changes-Close-button-scaled.webp)

Your links now appear in the top navbar of the course, ready for students to use.

![Preview of course links in the top navbar](/images/course-and-learning/course-links/8.-preview-of-course-link.webp)
