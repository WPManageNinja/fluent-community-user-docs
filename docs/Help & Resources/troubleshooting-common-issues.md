# Troubleshooting Common Issues

Even with the best tools, sometimes things don't go as planned. This guide provides solutions to some of the most common issues users encounter with FluentCommunity.

## My community portal looks unstyled or different from my theme.

By default, the FluentCommunity portal uses its own modern, app-like styling to ensure a consistent and fast experience. It is designed to be theme-agnostic.

* **Solution:** While it won't inherit every detail from your theme, you can add your own branding (logo, colors) in the settings. For deeper visual changes, you can use the **Custom CSS** feature to override the default styles and match your theme more closely.

## I enabled a Pro module, but the feature isn't appearing.

If you have enabled a Pro module like Gamification or Courses but don't see the options on the front-end, there are a few things to check.

* **Check Your License:** Ensure your FluentCommunity Pro license is active and correctly entered in your site's settings. An expired license can sometimes disable pro features.
* **Clear Your Cache:** If you're using a caching plugin, try clearing your cache to see if the feature appears.
