# Analytics (Pro)

Understanding how your community is performing is essential for sustainable growth. The Analytics section provides you with the data-driven insights you need to monitor key metrics and understand user behavior.

This section of the documentation covers the built-in reporting tools that help you get a clear picture of your community's health.

### What You Will Learn

In the following articles, we will explore the different reports available:

* **The Analytics Dashboard:** Get a high-level overview of your most important community statistics in one central place.
* **Tracking Community Growth:** Learn how to use specific charts to monitor new member acquisition and identify trends over time.

- Explore [content moderation](../community-hub/content-moderation.md) to maintain quality
