# In App Notification Settings

The **Notification Center** is a key feature for keeping your members engaged. It provides real-time, on-site alerts for important activities, ensuring users never miss a conversation that is relevant to them. This guide covers how it works and the options available.

## How In-App Notifications Work

Located in the top navigation bar of the community portal, the notification icon (often a "bell") will display a badge with a number when a member has unread notifications.

[Screenshot: The top navigation bar of the community, with the notification bell icon showing a red badge.]

Clicking this icon opens the Notification Center, which shows a list of recent activities, such as:
* When someone **mentions** you in a post or comment.
* When someone **replies** to one of your posts or comments.
* When a new member **joins** a space you are in.
* When you receive a new **badge** or level up.

This immediate feedback loop encourages members to return to conversations and stay active on the platform.

## Configuring Notification Preferences

While global notification events are set by the administrator, individual members have control over their own notification preferences. This allows them to tailor the alerts to their liking.

Each member can typically access their own notification settings from their profile page. Here, they can choose which types of events they wish to be notified about.
