# Color Customization

FluentCommunity lets you customize the color of almost every part of your portal, including the **Navbar**, **Sidebar**, **Feed**, **Content**, **Links**, **Text**, and **Buttons**. The interface supports both **Light** and **Dark** themes, and you can style each mode independently so your community looks great either way.

> **Use Case:** You want your community portal to match your brand's palette, with a bright, airy look in Light mode and a comfortable, low-glare scheme in Dark mode.

## How to Customize Colors

1.  Go to the **Settings** page of your portal.
2.  Click the **Customize Colors** button in the top-right corner.

![The Customize Colors button in the top-right of the Settings page](/images/portal-settings/color-customization/1.-Customize-Colors-button-scaled.webp)

### Light Mode

When you open the color customizer, you land on the **Light Mode** tab by default.

1.  Click the **Arrow** icon inside the **Color Schema** box to open a dropdown of color options.
2.  Choose any **pre-built color template**, or select **Custom** to define your own colors.
3.  Click **Save Settings** to apply your changes.

![The Light Mode Color Schema dropdown field](/images/portal-settings/color-customization/2.-Light-Mode-Color-Schema-field.webp)

When you choose **Custom**, three groups of color options become available:

* **Header:** Colors for the top navigation bar and its elements.
* **Sidebar:** Colors for the left navigation sidebar.
* **General:** Colors for the feed, content, links, text, and buttons.

### Dark Mode

To style the dark theme, switch to the **Dark Mode** tab in the color customizer. The process is exactly the same as Light Mode: pick a pre-built template or choose **Custom** to set the **Header**, **Sidebar**, and **General** colors, then click **Save Settings**.
