# Communication

Keeping your members informed and engaged is vital for a healthy community. This section covers the tools FluentCommunity uses to communicate with your users automatically.

These settings allow you to control how your platform sends alerts for important activities like new replies, mentions, and other community events.

### What You Will Learn

In the following articles, we will explore the different communication channels:

* **In-App Notification Settings:** Learn about the real-time, on-site notification center that alerts users to direct interactions.
* **Customizing Email Templates (Pro):** Discover how to edit and brand the automated emails that are sent to your members.
