# Creating & Managing Polls

Polls are a quick way to connect with your community and gather their feedback. FluentCommunity lets you create polls right from your feed and collect valuable insights from your members in just a few clicks.

> **Use Case:** You're planning your next live workshop and can't decide on a topic. Post a poll asking members to vote between three options, set an end date, and let the community shape your content calendar for you.

## How to Create a Poll from the Global Feed

1.  From your FluentCommunity dashboard, open the **Global Feed**.
2.  Click the postcard where you normally create posts.
3.  In the options that appear, click the **Poll** icon to open the poll creation features.

![The Global Feed postcard with the Poll icon](/images/community-hub/creating-and-managing-polls/FluentCommunity-Poll-1-scaled.webp)

### Configure Your Poll

* **Topic Section:** Give your poll a topic name.
* **Subject Section:** Enter your poll question or any text content you want to share.
* **Topic:** If you're creating the poll from within a specific Space, a **Topic Add** button lets you attach a topic you've previously saved for that Space.
* **Poll Options:** Add as many poll options as you like. To remove one, click the **cross (X)** icon next to it.
* **Add More:** Click the **Add More** button to add another option.
* **Poll Settings:** Check the **User can choose multiple options** checkbox to let members select more than one answer.
* **End Date:** Set how long the poll stays open. Click the date field and pick your desired date from the calendar.
* **Select Space:** Choose which Space to post your poll in. This option appears only when you create a poll from the Global Feed — it won't show when you start from within a specific Space.
* **Send announcement email to space member:** Check this box to email every member of the selected Space when the poll goes live, so no one misses the chance to vote.
* **Post:** Click the **Post** button to publish your poll.
* **Cancel:** Click **Cancel** to discard the poll.

![The poll creation options panel](/images/community-hub/creating-and-managing-polls/Poll-options-scaled.webp)

## How to Create a Poll from a Space

You can create a poll from a [specific Space](/community-hub/creating-a-new-space) in the same way. Head to the Space where you want the poll, click the **Poll** button, and configure the rest of the settings exactly as above.
