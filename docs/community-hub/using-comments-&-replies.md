# Using Comments & Replies

Comments are where the conversation happens. They allow your members to engage directly with posts, ask questions, and share their thoughts. FluentCommunity supports both top-level comments and nested replies, creating organized discussion threads.


## How to Add a Comment

Any member with access to a post can contribute to the discussion.

* **Locate the Input Box:** Below any post in the feed, you will find a text box labeled "Write a comment...".
* **Compose Your Message:** Click inside the box and type your thoughts.
* **Add Media and Expressions:** You can attach images, GIFs, or use emojis to make your comment more engaging.
* **Publish:** Once you are ready, click the **Post Comment** button to publish your response.

Your comment will immediately appear below the post.

  ![Add a Comment](/images/community-hub/using-comment-and-replies/add-a-comment-1.webp)

## How to Reply to a Comment

To keep conversations organized, you can reply directly to a specific comment, which creates a nested thread.

* **Find the Comment:** Locate the specific contribution you want to address.
* **Click Reply:** Select the **Reply** link located directly beneath that comment.
* **Type and Send:** An input box will appear for your response; type your message and click the send icon to publish.

Nested replies ensure that the context of the conversation remains clear for all members.

  ![Comment Reply](/images/community-hub/using-comment-and-replies/reply-to-comment-2.webp)

## Advanced Discussion Features

FluentCommunity offers additional tools to help you navigate and manage deeper conversations:

* **View More Comments:** For posts with extensive discussions, click the **View more comments** link to open a dedicated popup modal.
* **Sort Discussions:** Inside the comment modal, you can sort the conversation by **Latest**, **Popular**, **Earliest**, or **Most Replied** to find the most relevant information.
* **Direct Links:** Click the three-dot menu on any comment and select **Copy Link** to share a specific part of the discussion with others.

Comments and replies are essential for building a vibrant, interactive community.
