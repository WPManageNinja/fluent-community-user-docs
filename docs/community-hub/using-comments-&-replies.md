# Using Comments & Replies

Comments are where the conversation happens. They allow your members to engage directly with posts, ask questions, and share their thoughts. FluentCommunity supports both top-level comments and nested replies, creating organized discussion threads.

## How to Add a Comment

Any member who can see a post can also comment on it.

1.  Below any post in a Space's feed, you will find a text box that says "Write a comment..."
2.  Click inside the box and type your message.
3.  You can also attach images or use emojis in your comments.
4.  Press **Enter** or click the **post comment** button.

Your comment will now appear below the post.

![Add a Comment](/images/community-hub/using-comment-and-replies/add-a-comment-1.webp)


## How to Reply to a Comment

To keep conversations organized, you can reply directly to a specific comment. This creates a nested thread.

1.  Find the comment you want to reply to.
2.  Click the **Reply** link directly beneath that comment.
3.  A new input box will appear, indented under the original comment.
4.  Type your reply and press **Enter** or click the **Reply**.

Your reply will appear nested under the original comment, creating a clear conversation thread.

Comments and replies are essential for community engagement.

![Reply a Comment](/images/community-hub/using-comment-and-replies/reply-comment-2.webp)

## Advanced Comment Management

FluentCommunity provides advanced options to help maintain community standards and highlight valuable discussions.

*  **Locate Your Comment**: Find your comment under the post you commented on.
* **Pin to Top**: Admins can keep the most important information visible by clicking the **three-dot (...)** button beside a comment and selecting **Pin to Top**. Pinned comments stay at the top of the discussion for all members to see.
* **Report Comment**: To help keep the community safe, members can click the **three-dot (...)** button and select **Report Comment** to flag inappropriate content for moderation review.
* **Edit**: Click the **three-dot (...)** menu and select **Edit** to update your comment's text or media.
* **Delete**: Click the **three-dot (...)** menu and choose **Delete** to permanently remove your comment.
* **View More Comments**: If a post has many responses, click the **View more comments** link to open a dedicated pop-up modal with the full discussion.

![Advanced Comment](/images/community-hub/using-comment-and-replies/advanced-comment-3.webp)

### Sorting Comments

Inside the pop-up modal, you can organize comments to find the most relevant content. Click the sorting dropdown to filter comments by:

* **Latest**
* **Popular**
* **Earliest**
* **Most Replied**
