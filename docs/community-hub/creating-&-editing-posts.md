# Creating & Editing Posts

Posts are the lifeblood of your community. They are the primary way for you and your members to share updates, ask questions, and start discussions. This guide covers how to create, format, and edit posts within a Space.

## How to Create a New Post

Any member of a Space can create a new post to engage with others.

 * **Navigate to the Space:** Go to the specific **Space** or the main community Feed where you want to share your content.
 * **Open the Editor:** At the top of the feed, you will see a content editor box labeled "What's happening, [Name]?". Click inside it to begin writing.
 * **Write Your Content:** Enter your message in the main text area.
 * **Add a Title (Optional):** You can include a title to help your post stand out and summarize the topic.
 * **Add Media and Attachments:** Use the icons at the bottom left of the editor to upload images, videos, or GIFs.
 * **Inline Media:** You can now insert **Inline Images** directly within the text area by using the block selector or typing `/` to select the **Image** option.
 * **Select a Space:** If you are posting from the main Feed, use the Posting in: Select Space dropdown to choose the correct destination for your post.
 * **Publish:** Once you are finished, click the **Post** button to publish it to the feed.

![Create a new Post](/images/community-hub/creating-and-editing/create-a-new-post-1.png)

### Formatting Your Posts

FluentCommunity uses a rich text editor, allowing you to format your posts for better clarity and emphasis. You can access formatting options by highlighting text or using the inline block menu:

 * **Headings:** Organize your content using **Heading 2, Heading 3, or Heading 4**.
 * **Lists:** Use **Bullet Lists** or **Ordered Lists** to make information easier to scan.
 * **Emphasis:** Apply **Bold** and **Italics** to key phrases.
 * **Special Blocks:** Add **Quotes** for citations or **Code Blocks** for technical snippets.

> **✨ Note:**
> Using formatting, especially headings and lists, is a great way to make longer posts easier to read and scan for other members.

  ![Formatting Post](/images/community-hub/creating-and-editing/formate-post-2.png)

### Share the Post

You can also share the post with the link. To get the post link click on the **three-dot** button and you will find the link.

  ![Share Post](/images/community-hub/creating-and-editing/share-link-3.png)

### Editing or Deleting a Post

If you need to make changes after publishing, you can easily modify or remove your content.

1.  Find the post you wish to edit in the Space's feed.
2.  Click the **three-dot menu (...)** at the top right corner of the post.
3.  Select **Edit** from the dropdown menu. This will re-open the post in the editor.
4.  Make your desired changes and click **Update Post**.

To remove a post permanently, you can select **Delete** from the same three-dot menu.

![Editing and Deleting Post](/images/community-hub/creating-and-editing/editing-and-delete-4.png)

> **✨ Note:**
> Only administrators and moderators have the additional ability to **Pin to Top** or manage reported content to maintain community standards.
