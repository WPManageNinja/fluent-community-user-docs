# Feed Views

The activity feed in FluentCommunity keeps members up to date on recent posts, comments, and other activity. It offers three view modes so everyone can browse the way they prefer: **Card View**, **List View**, and **Timeline View**. Here's how each one works and how to switch between them.

> **Use Case:** Your community shares a lot of visual, image-heavy posts. Switching to **Card View** lets members scan updates as a grid of thumbnails, while a text-focused group might prefer the compact **List View** instead.

To switch between views, click the **View icon** button at the top of the activity feed and choose the layout you want.

## Card View

* **Layout:** A structured grid that displays each activity as an individual card.
* **Best for:** Members who like a visual grid to scan content at a glance.

Card View is also available as a default layout option in the [**Space Settings**](/space-settings-&-privacy) section.

![Card View layout of the activity feed](/images/community-hub/feed-views/card-view.webp)

## List View

* **Layout:** A simple, compact list of activities in chronological order.
* **Best for:** Members who prefer a straightforward, streamlined layout with quick access to liking, commenting, and sharing directly from the feed.

![List View layout of the activity feed](/images/community-hub/feed-views/List-View-Feed--scaled.webp)

## Timeline View

* **Layout:** A visually organized timeline grouped by date, highlighting posts and activity.
* **Best for:** Members who enjoy expanded previews and want to browse activity by date.

![Timeline View layout of the activity feed](/images/community-hub/feed-views/Content-View-Feed--scaled.webp)

Each view lets members choose the style that best fits how they like to keep up with a Space's activity.
