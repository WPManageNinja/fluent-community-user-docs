# Following & Blocking Members

The Follow & Block module adds a layer of social interaction to your community, letting members connect and personalize their experience much like they would on a social network. When enabled, members can **follow** users whose content they enjoy to build a curated "Following" feed, and **block** others for a safer, more controlled environment.

> **Use Case:** A member wants a quieter feed focused on the handful of experts they trust. They follow those users to build a personalized feed and block a disruptive account to stop seeing its posts entirely, all without anyone being notified.

## How to Set Up the Module (For Admins)

The module is disabled by default, so you'll activate and configure it first.

### Step 1: Open the Follower Module Settings

1.  From your WordPress dashboard, go to **FluentCommunity → Portal Settings** and click the **Features & Addons** tab.
2.  Find the **Follower Module** in the list of Advanced Features.
3.  Click the **Settings** button next to it.

![The Features & Addons tab with the Follower Module settings button](/images/community-hub/following-and-blocking-members/follow-module-1-scaled.webp)

### Step 2: Configure and Save

A **Follower Module Settings** window opens. Configure the following:

* **Enable Follower Module:** Check this box to turn the entire feature on.
* **Who can see the followers list:** Choose who can see the list of users who follow a member.
* **Who can see the followings list:** Choose who can see the list of users a member is following.

For both privacy options, you can choose:

* **Everyone:** Any visitor to your site.
* **Members Only:** Only logged-in community members.
* **Only Profile Owner and Admin:** The list stays private to the user and site administrators.

Click the **Save Settings** button to finalize your configuration.

![The Follower Module Settings window with privacy options](/images/community-hub/following-and-blocking-members/follow-module-2-scaled.webp)

## How Members Use the Feature

### Following and Unfollowing Users

Members can follow others to see their posts and updates more easily.

* **How to Follow:** A **Follow** button appears on other members' [profile pages](/community-hub/the-member-directory) and on their member cards in the [Member Directory](/community-hub/the-member-directory). Clicking it makes them a follower.
* **Notifications:** Use the notification icon to control notifications, so the followed user is alerted about new posts or comments.
* **How to Unfollow:** Once you follow someone, the button changes to **Following**. Click it again to unfollow.

![The Follow button on a member's profile](/images/community-hub/following-and-blocking-members/follow-module-4-scaled.webp)

### Viewing Following & Follower Lists

Every member's profile page includes two tabs: **Following** and **Followers**. Their visibility to other users depends on the privacy settings you configured when enabling the module.

![The Following and Followers tabs on a member profile](/images/community-hub/following-and-blocking-members/follow-module-7.webp)

### Filtering Your Feed by "Following"

To see a personalized feed with posts only from members you follow:

1.  Go to the main **Feed** page.
2.  Click the **Sort by:** dropdown above the posts.
3.  Select **Following** from the list.

Your feed instantly updates to show your curated view.

![The Sort by dropdown with the Following filter selected](/images/community-hub/following-and-blocking-members/following-filter-feed-scaled.webp)

### Blocking and Unblocking Users

For added privacy and safety, members can block other users. The block is silent — the other person is **not** notified.

* **How to Block:** On any member's profile page, click the **three-dot** menu next to the **Follow** button, then select **Block**. Once blocked, you'll no longer see that member's posts.

![The three-dot menu with the Block option on a member profile](/images/community-hub/following-and-blocking-members/follow-module-5-scaled.webp)

**How to Unblock:** Members can manage everyone they've blocked from one place.

1.  Go to the main **Members** page of the community.
2.  Click the **Blocked** tab.
3.  Review everyone you've blocked and unblock them from here.

![The Blocked tab listing blocked members](/images/community-hub/following-and-blocking-members/follow-module-6-scaled.webp)
