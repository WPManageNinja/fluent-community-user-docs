# Membership Invitations

FluentCommunity gives you a dedicated **Invitations** feature so you can generate special links that invite new members straight into a specific **Space**. This guide walks you through creating, tracking, and sharing those invitation links.

> **Use Case:** You're running a special promotion and want to invite exactly 50 people to your VIP Space. You can create a link titled "Promo 50," cap it at 50 uses, and set it to expire on Friday, which builds urgency while keeping the group exclusive.

Before you begin, make sure you're logged into your WordPress dashboard and that you have **Administrator** or **Moderator** privileges for the Space you want to invite members to.

## How to Send Membership Invitations

### Step 1: Open the Invitations Menu

First, open the invitation dashboard for the Space you want to grow.

1.  From your community portal, select **Spaces** in the main top navigation.
2.  Find the Space you want to grow and click the **View Space** button.

![The Spaces page with the View Space button](/images/community-hub/membership-invitations/member-invitation-1-scaled.webp)

3.  Inside the Space, click the **Members** tab in the top-right menu.
4.  Click the **Invitations** button, located right next to the [**Add Member**](/community-hub/adding-members-to-a-space) button.

![The Members tab with the Invitations button](/images/community-hub/membership-invitations/member-invitation-2-scaled.webp)

### Step 2: Track Your Active Invitations

When you open the Invitations area, you'll land on the **Invitations** tab. This is your command center for tracking the status of every invite you've sent.

* **Invitee:** Lists the invitation links you've created or the specific users you've invited.
* **Invited By:** Shows the profile of the host or admin who generated the invitation.
* **Status:** Tells you at a glance whether a link is **Pending**, **Accepted**, **Active**, or **Expired**.
* **Actions:** Delete any specific invitation from the list to keep things tidy.
* **Filter:** Use the dropdown in the top-right to filter the list by status, so you can quickly find expired or pending links.

![The Invitations dashboard showing invitation statuses](/images/community-hub/membership-invitations/member-invitation-3-scaled.webp)

### Step 3: Create a New Invitation Link

Generating a shareable link is the easiest way to invite a group of people at once.

1.  From the Invitations dashboard, click the **New Invitation Link** tab.
2.  **Invitation Link Title:** Give your link a clear name (e.g., "Spring 2026 VIP Invite") so you can track it later.
3.  **Limit the number of uses:** Enter a number to cap how many people can use this link, or leave it empty for unlimited uses.
4.  **Expire Date:** Click the calendar field to set a date for the link to stop working, or leave it empty to keep the link active permanently.
5.  Click the **Create Invitation Link** button.

![The New Invitation Link form](/images/community-hub/membership-invitations/member-invitation-4-scaled.webp)

Once the link is created, copy it and share it via email, social media, or direct message with the people you want to add.

![The created invitation link ready to copy and share](/images/community-hub/membership-invitations/member-invitation-5-scaled.webp)

> **✨ Note:**
> When someone clicks your invitation link, the system handles the rest. If they're already logged into your community, they're instantly added to the Space. If they're brand new, they're prompted with a friendly [Login/Signup form](/portal-settings/customizing-the-login-signup-form) to create their account before joining.

![The Login or Signup form shown to new members](/images/community-hub/membership-invitations/10.-Login-or-Signup-form-.webp)
