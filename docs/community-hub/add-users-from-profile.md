# Add Users to Spaces & Courses from a Profile

FluentCommunity allows you to manage member enrollments directly from a user's individual profile. This article covers the process of granting access to specific Spaces or Courses without leaving the [member management area](./the-member-directory.md).

::: warning Admins only
Only Administrators can add members to **Spaces** or **Courses** directly from their profile pages.
:::

## Enrolling Members into Spaces or Courses

To enroll a member in a new Space or Course from their profile, follow these instructions.

First, navigate to the specific [**User Profile**](./managing-user-profiles.md) and select the **Spaces & Courses** tab to view their current list of joined areas.

Click the **+ Add to Space / Course** button located at the top right of the enrollment list.

![The Spaces & Courses tab on a user profile with the Add to Space / Course button](/images/community-hub/add-users-from-profile/add-user-1-scaled.webp)

An **Add User to Spaces/Courses** pop-up modal will appear on your screen. Select the desired **Space/Course** from the dropdown options.

After selecting the space, choose the [**Membership Role**](./assigning-user-roles.md) (such as Member, Moderator, or Admin) from the corresponding dropdown option. Click the **Add to Space** button to finalize the enrollment.

Once you click the button, the new Space or Course will immediately appear in the user's enrollment list.

![The Add User to Spaces/Courses modal with space and role dropdowns](/images/community-hub/add-users-from-profile/add-spaces-member-2.webp)
