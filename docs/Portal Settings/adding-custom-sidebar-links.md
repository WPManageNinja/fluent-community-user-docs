# Adding Custom Sidebar Links

The main sidebar in your community portal provides the primary navigation for your members. FluentCommunity allows you to add your own custom links to this sidebar, making it easy to direct users to important resources, whether they are inside or outside your community.

> **Use Case:** You can add a "Read Our Blog" link that points to your main company blog, a "My Account" link that takes users to their WooCommerce account page, and a "Community Guidelines" link that directs to a page explaining your rules.

## How to Add a Custom Sidebar Link

1.  From your WordPress dashboard, navigate to **FluentCommunity → Settings**.
2.  Click on the **Layout & Appearance** or a similarly named tab.
3.  Find the section for **Custom Sidebar Links**.
4.  Click the **Add New Link** button.

This will reveal a set of fields to configure your new link.

[Screenshot: The Custom Sidebar Links settings area, with the "Add New Link" button highlighted.]

## Configuring Your Link

For each link you add, you need to provide the following details:

* **Link Title:** This is the text that will be displayed in the sidebar for your link (e.g., "Premium Support").
* **URL:** The full web address that the link should point to. This can be an internal page on your WordPress site or any external website.
* **Icon (Optional):** You may be able to select an icon to display next to your link title for a more visual touch.
* **Open in New Tab:** Check this box if you want the link to open in a new browser tab. This is highly recommended for external links.

[Screenshot: The fields for configuring a new sidebar link, showing Title, URL, Icon selection, and the "Open in New Tab" checkbox.]

You can add multiple custom links. They can also be reordered by dragging and dropping them into your desired position.

Click the **Save Settings** button to apply your changes. Your new custom links will now be visible to all members in the community portal sidebar.
