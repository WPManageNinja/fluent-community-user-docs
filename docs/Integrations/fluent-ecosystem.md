# Fluent Ecosystem

FluentCommunity is designed to be a powerful standalone platform, but its capabilities are greatly enhanced when used with other plugins from the Fluent family. This shared ecosystem allows for deep, seamless integrations that enable powerful automation and a more unified administrative experience.

This section covers how to connect FluentCommunity with other "Fluent" plugins.

### What You Will Learn

In the following articles, we will explore how to connect and use these key integrations:

* **Connecting with FluentCRM:** Automate your email marketing by tagging users and triggering funnels based on their community activity.
* **Connecting with Fluent Forms:** Create custom registration forms and automatically enroll users into specific Spaces or Courses.
* **Connecting with Fluent Support:** Provide your support agents with instant context by displaying a user's community and course details directly within their support tickets.
* **Connecting with Paymattic:** Monetize your community for free by selling access to your premium Spaces and Courses with a powerful, flexible payment solution.

**Get Started:**
- Learn about [creating spaces](/creating-a-new-space) to organize your community
- Explore [course creation](/creating-your-first-course) for learning content
- Set up [gamification](/configuring-the-points-system) to encourage engagement
