# Connecting With Fluentcrm

Integrating FluentCommunity with **FluentCRM** unlocks powerful automation capabilities, allowing you to bridge the gap between your community engagement and your email marketing efforts. This connection lets you tag users in FluentCRM based on their community actions and run automations when they join spaces or complete courses.

> **Use Case:** You can create an automation where a new member who joins your "Beginner's Course" space is automatically added to a "New Students" list in FluentCRM and receives a welcome email sequence with tips on how to get started.

## How the Integration Works

The integration is designed to be seamless, as both plugins are part of the same ecosystem. Once connected, a new set of options will become available within FluentCRM's automation builder.

## Step 1: Enable the Integration

First, ensure both FluentCommunity and FluentCRM are installed and activated on your WordPress site. The integration is enabled by default.

## Step 2: Using FluentCommunity Triggers in FluentCRM

The primary power of this integration comes from the new triggers available in FluentCRM's automation funnels.

1.  From your WordPress dashboard, navigate to **FluentCRM → Automations**.
2.  Create a new automation or edit an existing one.
3.  When selecting a new trigger to start the funnel, you will see a **FluentCommunity** section.

[Screenshot: The FluentCRM automation trigger selection screen, with the "FluentCommunity" trigger category highlighted.]

### Available Triggers

You can start an automation based on specific community events, such as:

* **User Joins a Space:** Triggers when a member joins any specific space.
* **User Completes a Course (Pro):** Triggers when a student marks a course as complete.
* **User Levels Up (Pro):** Triggers when a member reaches a new level in your gamification system.

### Available Actions

Within an automation, you can also perform actions back on the user in FluentCommunity.

* **Add To Space:** Add a contact to a specific Space.
* **Remove From Space:** Remove a contact from a specific Space.
